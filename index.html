Degenerate is a hi-fi and acoustics company creating spaces for music, sound, and culture.

We have decades of experience designing custom audio systems tailored to the unique budgets, needs, and tastes of our clients.

Our shop space includes three dedicated listening rooms, acoustically treated and equipped with state of the art streamers, speakers, and electronics.

It also doubles as an intimate rehearsal studio, live venue, and community space for some of the most talented artists and musicians in Puerto Rico.
